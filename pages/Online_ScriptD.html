POST EXAM INFORMATION

  • NEW: We will be emailing you a CSCE. Once the FCC receives your exam information, they will email you a link with payment instructions. You have 10 days to make the payment directly to the FCC. Making changes may delay or void your application.

  • UPGRADE: We will email you a CSCE with instructions on how to verify your upgrade online. That process should be completed by the end of next week.
